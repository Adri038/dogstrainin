About Us

I have loved animals all my life. When I was a child, I rode horses on play days and rodeos, showed steers in 4-H; but I always had a dog. Dogs are great animals, they give their love so freely. My new career; Canine obedience training is a goal I had always set for myself, but never set the time. Now, as I move toward my goal, I feel encouraged by the learning I have received, I can see the dog in its truest form: a helper and companion. Now I see how important it is to have a well-behaved dog in our society: whether flying, going to the dog park, or simply walking down the street; Well-behaved dogs are noticeable. This gives the owner pride and more enjoyment of the dog. Our dogs want to respect us and follow our commands, the pack rules, follow the leader. Now is your time to teach your dog to follow you!

I am a certified professional trainer with memberships in leading canine training organizations and certifications in positive reinforcement training methods.
